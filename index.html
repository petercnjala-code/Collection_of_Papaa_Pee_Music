About Papaa Pee

Papaa Pee is a Malawian artist blending Afrobeat rhythms with cultural storytelling. At 52, he continues to inspire through music and environmental studies, working as a Quality Assurance Technician at MBS.

Musical Inspiration

Influenced by legends like Bob Marley and Fela Kuti, Papaa Pee’s music carries messages of resilience, love, and community.

Projects

From geospatial analysis to songwriting, Papaa Pee merges science and art. His projects highlight environmental awareness while celebrating African rhythms.
